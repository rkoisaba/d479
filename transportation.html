Getting Around Taniti

Taniti offers a variety of transportation options to help you explore the island with ease:

  • Car Rentals: Available at the airport and in the main city. A valid driver’s license is required.
  • Public Buses: Affordable and convenient, with routes covering major attractions and towns.
  • Taxis: Taxis are readily available in the city and at the airport. Most drivers speak English.
  • Bicycle Rentals: Available in tourist areas for those who prefer a more eco-friendly way to explore the island.
  • Ferries: Regular ferry services connect the main island to nearby smaller islands, perfect for day trips.

For more detailed information on schedules and pricing, visit our transportation service providers' websites or contact your hotel concierge.
